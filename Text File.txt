30. Mocking:
	-What is mocking?:
		-Override or change the behaviour of testing
		-Avoid unintended side effects
		-Isolate code being tested
	-Why use mocking?
		-Avoid relying on external services
			-Can't guranttee they will be available
			-Makes test upredictable and inconsistent
		-Avoid unintended consequencies
			-Accidentally sending emails
			-Overloading external services
